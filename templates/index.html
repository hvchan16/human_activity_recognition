Human Activity Recognition!

Recognition actions of a person using the webcam of the client and also visualizing the probablity of tha action performed using libraries; Mediapipe, OpenCV and LSTM Model.

Human activity recognition is present in daily contact and interpersonal relationships. It is tough to extract since it includes information about a person's identity, personality, and psychological condition. One of the primary themes of research in the scientific fields of computer vision and machine learning is the human ability to identify the actions of others. As a consequence of this study, numerous applications, such as video surveillance systems, human-computer interface, and robots for human behaviour characterization, require a multimodal activity detection system.
